Featured Published: October 2025

Intravenous Administration of Muse Cells for Acute Ischemic Stroke: A Phase 2 Double-Blind Trial

Kuroda Y, Dezawa M, et al. | The Lancet Neurology

This pivotal study demonstrates the profound neurorestorative capabilities of Muse cells when administered intravenously within 24 hours of acute ischemic stroke onset. Patients exhibited significant functional recovery compared to the control group, with robust evidence of cellular homing to the infarcted region and structural integration without tumorigenesis.
